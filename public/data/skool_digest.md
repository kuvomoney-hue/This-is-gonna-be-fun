# the creative tech digest

hey everyone. starting something new here. every week or so, I'll drop a roundup of what's actually moving in the creative space. ai tools, camera gear, editing software, whatever matters if you're making things.

this one covers the last three weeks (Mar 27 to Apr 03, 2026). buckle up.

---

## the big shift: local ai is real now

alibaba just committed to [keeping qwen open source](https://i.redd.it/7jg4cgd0kyrg1.png). this matters because qwen is the thing that runs on your laptop, not in the cloud. people are running studio quality ai on macbooks and even decade old computers now.

what this means for you: no subscription fees, no sending your work to someone else's servers, no waiting. the tools live on your machine.

---

## video ai just got way better

[ltx 2.3 dropped](https://www.reddit.com/r/StableDiffusion/comments/1sajk80/ltx_desktop_103_is_live_now_runs_on_16_gb_vram/) and the entire community noticed. better textures, sharper details, native portrait mode. they even shipped a free desktop app for it.

people are making stuff like this now:
- [ltx 2.3 reasoning vbvr lora comparison on facial expressions](https://v.redd.it/jrykwj1vfzrg1)
- [i got ltx-2.3 running in real-time on a 4090](https://v.redd.it/itm7c81ntnrg1)

what this means for you: video generation isn't "coming soon" anymore. it's here, and you can run it at home if you have a decent gpu.

---

## iphone just became a real video camera

[kino](https://petapixel.com/2026/04/02/reeflex-pro-camera-3-0-brings-dslr-style-controls-to-iphone/) (the video app from the halide team) added apple log 2 support. this is the same color profile professional cameras use. your iphone can now shoot footage that matches your cinema camera.

what this means for you: b roll, behind the scenes, even a cam shots just got way more flexible. color grade everything together without fighting the iphone footage.
